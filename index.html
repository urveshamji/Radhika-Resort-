Our Story

A Beachside
Paradise in Diu


Nestled along the pristine shores of Nagoa Beach, Radhika Beach Resort & Spa is Diu's most cherished escape. Located on the sun-drenched island of Diu — a union territory off Gujarat's Kathiawar peninsula — we offer an unmatched blend of Portuguese-era charm and modern luxury.

Whether you're here for the golden sunsets, the warm Arabian Sea, or simply to unwind with world-class spa treatments, every moment at Radhika is crafted to leave you refreshed, restored, and enchanted.

Our resort is perfectly positioned to explore Diu's historic forts, tranquil churches, and vibrant seafood culture — while always having a serene retreat to return to.
